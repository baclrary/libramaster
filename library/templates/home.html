User-Friendly Interface

Book Reservation Feature

Allows patrons to effortlessly reserve books online. Users can browse the library's collection, check availability, and place reservations directly from their account. Library staff is notified to set aside reserved items, while patrons receive notifications when their books are ready for pickup.

Domain white list

LibraMaster's registration process includes a domain whitelist check to prevent users with temporary or disposable email addresses from accessing our platform. This ensures a secure and trustworthy environment by deterring spam, fraud, and misuse, allowing us to focus on providing a high-quality experience for genuine patrons and library staff.

Adaptive search

We offer a simple yet effective adaptive search feature, allowing users to search across multiple fields simultaneously. This ensures a more comprehensive search experience, enabling patrons and staff to quickly locate the desired resources. The straightforward approach saves time and enhances the overall library experience for all users.

Manage Orders

Our site offers a streamlined and user-friendly ordering process for guests. With our easy-to-use interface, guests can quickly place orders and make modifications as needed.
